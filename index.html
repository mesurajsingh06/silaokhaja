The Heritage of Silao Town

Our operational workspace remains rooted in Silao, a historical locality sitting smoothly between the archaeological ruins of Rajgir and ancient Nalanda. Here, families belonging directly to the traditional Sah community collective have preserved the intricate art of hand-rolling layered wheat pastries without standard machinery modifications since 320 BCE.

True luxury is defined by environmental constraints that cannot be built synthetically elsewhere. The signature structural puffiness of real Silao Khaja depends heavily on local underground well streams carrying an anomalously high pH signature.
