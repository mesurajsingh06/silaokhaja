The Heritage

The Silao Lore & Secrets

Silao vs Odisha: The Tale of Two Custodians

While Odisha’s Puri Khaja is thick and dense to withstand long temple journeys, our authentic **Silao Khaja** is completely light and flaky. Our artisans roll sheets so incredibly thin that the sweet melts almost instantly on your tongue.

Is a Sweet Healthy? The Real Ingredients

True traditional luxury uses clean ingredients. By discarding heavy industrial fats and using real desi ghee and local heirloom wheat, our sweet doesn't weigh down the stomach. For diabetic tracking, our savory batch drops the syrup entirely.

A Royal Blessing Dating to 320 BCE

Long before earning its official legal GI Tag in 2018, this multi-layered delicacy welcomed kings and sages. Local records show that as Lord Buddha walked between Rajgir and early Nalanda, the townspeople honored him with this very pastry.

The Hand-Crafted Art of 52 Layers

Affectionately known as the *Bawan Parat* style, creating 52 distinct sheets requires hours of skilled hand-rolling. When dropped into pure ghee, the mineral properties of our native alkaline well water cause the layers to puff open elegantly.
