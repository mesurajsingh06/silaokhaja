The Silao Lore & Secrets

Silao vs Odisha: The Tale of Two Custodians

While Odisha’s Puri Khaja is thick, firm, and dense to withstand long temple journeys, our authentic **Silao Khaja** is its opposite. It is completely light and flaky. Our artisans roll sheets so incredibly thin that the sweet melts almost instantly on your tongue, celebrating the lightness of pure ghee.

Is a Sweet Healthy? The Real Ingredients

True traditional luxury uses clean ingredients. By discarding heavy industrial fats and using real desi ghee and local heirloom wheat, our sweet doesn't weigh down the stomach. For those balancing their sugar levels, our handcrafted savory batch offers a traditional treat without the sugar syrup.

A Royal Blessing Dating to 320 BCE

Long before earning its official legal GI Tag in 2018, this multi-layered delicacy welcomed kings and sages. Ancient local records show that as Lord Buddha walked between Rajgir and the historic monasteries of Nalanda, the people of Silao honored his journey with this very pastry.

The Hand-Crafted Art of 52 Layers

Affectionately known by our elders as the *Bawan Parat* style, creating 52 distinct sheets requires hours of skilled hand-rolling. When dropped into pure ghee, the natural mineral properties of our native alkaline well water cause the layers to puff open elegantly.
